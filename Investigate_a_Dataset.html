This is obvious, because kids tend to catch cold easily and it would take more time for them to get better unlike the adults.On the other hand elder patients are the ones most likely to miss their scheduled appointments which makes sense as they may not feel well enough to take the trip to the hospital.

Conclusions¶

Age tends to be a major factor in determining the frequencey of successful visits by a patient after making an appointment. The Neighbourhood with the most number of patient visits is 'JARDIM CAMBURI' with 6154 successful visits on the scheduled appointments and this varies based on the Neighbourhood drastically. Also, other medical factors obviously play a major role for predicting the patient visits.

Graphs depicting these results have been plotted using required functions.

The dataset given is clearly limited as important data like the actual location of the hospital premises(compared with the patients' neighbourhood can determine how the distribution changes based on the distance b/w patients' residence and hospital campus) and individual consultant specific appointment details were not defined clearly which can be used in additional data exploration.
